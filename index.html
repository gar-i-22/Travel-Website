Five Great Places To Visit in NY!

  1. Statue of Liberty

    The Statue Of Liberty stands tall in NYC, as a gift from France to America. To get a breathtaking view of this statue take a ride to Liberty Island.

  2. Empire State Building

    The Empire State Building which is 102 stories tall, is located Midtown Manhattan. This large structure is the seventh largest building in the world.

  3. Central Park

    Central Park, covering 843 acres, is an urban park between the Upper West and Upper East Sides of Manhattan.

  4. Times Square

    The Times Square is a big entertainment center, attraction, and intersection. This cross is brightly lit by billboards and advertisements.

  5. Brooklyn Bridge

    The Brooklyn Bridge connects Manhattan and Long Island with a span of 1,595.5 feet.
